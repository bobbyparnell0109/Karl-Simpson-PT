Who Is Karl?

Karl is a King's Lynn-based personal trainer operating from Snap Fitness. He builds programmes around each individual client — whether you're recovering from injury, returning to fitness after years away, building strength from scratch, or pushing past a plateau.

He adjusts sessions based on how you're feeling, explains the why behind every exercise, and tracks your progress over time. No copy-paste programmes. No guesswork.
